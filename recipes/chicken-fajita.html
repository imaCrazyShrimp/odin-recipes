Chicken Fajita

Description

A vibrant, sizzling Mexican-inspired dish featuring seasoned chicken strips tossed with sweet bell peppers and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts (sliced into thin strips)
  • 2 bell peppers (any color, sliced into strips)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas for serving

Steps

  • In a small bowl, mix together the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss the chicken strips in half of the seasoning blend and a tablespoon of olive oil.
  • Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the chicken, and cook until it is no longer pink and slightly browned (about 5–7 minutes), then remove it from the pan.
  • Add the remaining olive oil to the same skillet, toss in the peppers and onions, sprinkle with the remaining spice blend, and sauté until tender-crisp.
  • Return the chicken to the skillet, toss everything together to warm through, and serve immediately alongside warm tortillas.
