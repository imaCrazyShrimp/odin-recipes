Broiled Steak

Description

A quick and high-heat cooking method that yields a beautifully seared crust and a juicy, tender interior.

Ingredients

  • 1 thick-cut steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  • Remove the steak from the refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking to bring it to room temperature.
  • Preheat your oven's broiler to high and set the oven rack about 4 to 6 inches below the heating element.
  • Pat the steak completely dry with paper towels, rub it with olive oil, and garlic powder.
  • Place the steak on a broiler pan or a cast-iron skillet.
  • Broil for about 4 to 6 minutes per side, depending on thickness, until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  • Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing against the grain to keep it juicy.
