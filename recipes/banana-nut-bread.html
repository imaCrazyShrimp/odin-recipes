Banana Nut Bread

Description

A classic, moist quick bread packed with sweet banana flavor and a crunchy texture from chopped nuts.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
