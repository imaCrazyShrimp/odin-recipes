Shokupan

Description

An incredibly soft, fluffy, and pillowy white bread made using the Tangzhong starter method to lock in moisture.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons softened unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons bread flour mixed with 6 tablespoons water (Tangzhong)
