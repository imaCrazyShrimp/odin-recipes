Curry

Description

A rich, fragrant, and deeply spiced stew that balances savory, sweet, and aromatic notes, perfect over a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs (cut into bite-sized pieces) or mixed vegetables
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  • Heat the cooking oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, sautéing until the onions are soft and translucent.
  • Stir in the curry powder and tomato paste, cooking for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add the chicken or vegetables to the pot, stirring well to coat them thoroughly in the spice mixture.
  • Pour in the coconut milk, bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, cover, and let it cook for 20 minutes until the protein or vegetables are completely tender.
