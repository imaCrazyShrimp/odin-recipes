Curry

Description

A rich, fragrant, and deeply spiced stew that balances savory, sweet, and aromatic notes, perfect over a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs (cut into bite-sized pieces) or mixed vegetables
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
